Periodic Table Facts

Hydrogen has the Symbol H.
Hydrogen has the Atomic Number 1.
Hydrogen has the Atomic Weight 1.00794.
Hydrogen is located in the periodic table in the S-Block.
Hydrogen has the colour colourless.

Helium has the Symbol He.
Helium has the Atomic Number 2.
Helium has the Atomic Weight 4.002602.
Helium is located in the periodic table in the P-Block.
Helium has the colour colourless.
Helium is in the group Noble Gas.

Lithium has the Symbol Li.
Lithium has the Atomic Number 3.
Lithium has the Atomic Weight 6.941.
Lithium is located in the periodic table in the S-Block.
Lithium has the colour silvery white/grey.
Lithium is in the group Alkali metal.

Beryllium has the Symbol Be.
Beryllium has the Atomic Number 4.
Beryllium has the Atomic Weight 9.012182.
Beryllium is located in the periodic table in the S-Block.
Beryllium has the colour lead grey.
Beryllium is in the group Alkaline earth metal.

Boron has the Symbol B.
Boron has the Atomic Number 5.
Boron has the Atomic Weight 10.811.
Boron is located in the periodic table in the P-Block.
Boron has the colour black.

Carbon has the Symbol C.
Carbon has the Atomic Number 6.
Carbon has the Atomic Weight 12.0107.
Carbon is located in the periodic table in the P-Block.
Carbon has the colour black as graphite, colourless as diamond.
